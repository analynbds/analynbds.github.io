About Me

I’m an upcoming graduate at the University of Hawaii at West Oahu with a Bachelors of Applied Science in Creative Media and a concentration in Interface Design. Previously I was at the University of Pennsylvania in Philadelphia.

I started my design journey in probably the most archaic fashion a designer could start in—newspaper design. My love for news and storytelling has allowed me to expand my skills into broader scopes in the past few years. Currently, I’m interested in tech and making meaningful products that can tell different types of stories.
