Photography

Ever since I took a photography class in 2014,

I could hardly stop myself from snapping away photos. I fell in love with being able to stop time and capture memories with a single click of a button. Out of curiousity, I joined my school's newspaper photo team where I learned the depth of journalistic photography and storytelling via a different medium beyond traditional design. I love photographing candid human interactions, and when I shoot landscape photos, I love pushing the boundaries between reality and fantasy. Stylistically, I don't quite aim to capture realism. Rather, I aim to capture and reveal exaggerated pieces that paint scenes for my future self to remember.
